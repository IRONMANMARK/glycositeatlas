N-GlycositeAtlas, A Mass Spectrometry-based Human Glycoprotein and Glycosite Database

N-GlycositeAtlas was assembled by combining more than 10,000 identified N-glycosylation sites from different biological sources including human-derived tissues, body fluids and cell lines from over 100 studies. You can download the whole database by FREE registration.
